    Cryptostorm isn't designed like the old-style, disloyal "VPN companies" - we've washed away needless complexity & distractions, for a direct focus on network security & cryptographic protection. Here's a few things that help folks get 'on-cstorm' quick & drama-free:

    Your token is all you use to connect to cryptostorm. There's no "account" & no extra info. Don't lose your token, as we can't replace it - we delete records of sold tokens, permanently. And don't worry about using your token right away - it won't begin 'eroding' towards expiration until you auth into cryptostorm the first time.

    Windows folks most often use our opensource network access 'widget' to connect to cryptostorm. The most-recent version is always available here & here. The widget automates most everything involved in making use of cryptostorm's service.

    Until we get the widget ported, members on other OSes find their connection tools in our howto guides (linux | android | iOS | OSX), or via quick links on our connect-info page. We support any platform that has enough cryptographic foundation to meet the needs of our security model. Most connections are quick and easy, right off the bat.
